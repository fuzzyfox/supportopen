Welcome to the Support Open... Tutorial!

This tutorial is meant to help you with this make, but if at any time you want to hide it, just click on the < arrow icon above.

Prior Knowledge

Some HTML and CSS is helpful, but not required. We'll provide step-by-step instructions here or you can find the bold and italicized prompts right in the code.

Change that Text

Let's start by changing the name of your webpage on line 9. This is optional, but feel free to personalize.

Why do you support open?

Head down to line 78 and tell us. Just replace the black text with your own words and see the change immediately in the preview panel on the right.

A Little About Yourself

Change the name on line 81 to be your own.

Don't forget to update the link to use your Webmaker username, or your location on line 82.

Add Your Avatar

Change the link on line 83 to link to your avatar (or any image from the web) and it will automagically be cropped with a round border.

Change the Background

You can change the background image by replacing the link on line 20.

Bonus Styling

Know CSS? Play around with the other colours and the text.

Congratulations!
